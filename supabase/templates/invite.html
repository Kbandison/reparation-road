About Reparation Road
Founded by Adam Jacoby Paul, Reparation Road is committed to making Black history accessible and empowering families to discover their heritage. Whether you're beginning your genealogical journey or are an experienced researcher, our platform provides the tools and resources you need.

Our mission is to uncover the stories that have been hidden, honor the ancestors who paved the way, and provide a comprehensive resource for understanding Black history in America.
